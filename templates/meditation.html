Meditation Guide

Relax your mind, reduce stress, and improve focus with these simple steps.

Step 1: Find a Quiet Place

Choose a calm environment where you won't be disturbed. Sit comfortably on a chair or cushion.

Step 2: Sit Comfortably

Keep your back straight, shoulders relaxed, and hands resting on your lap or knees. Close your eyes gently.

Step 3: Focus on Your Breath

Take slow, deep breaths. Pay attention to the inhale and exhale, letting your body relax with each breath.

Step 4: Observe Your Thoughts

Notice your thoughts without judging them. When your mind wanders, gently return focus to your breathing.

Step 5: End the Session

After 5-10 minutes, slowly open your eyes. Stretch gently and feel refreshed and calm.

Extra Tips

  • Practice daily to build a consistent routine.
  • Start with short sessions and gradually increase the duration.
  • You can listen to soft background music or nature sounds.
  • Be patient; meditation improves focus over time.
