About Us

This project is developed by four dedicated B.Tech CSE third-year students with the aim of building a functional and user-friendly platform for mental health tracking and support.

Our Aim: To create a platform that allows users to track their mental health, interact safely, and seek support when needed, fostering awareness and wellbeing among students and the wider community.
